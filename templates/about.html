About Pokégear

Conceived as part of a class project, Pokégear grew from frustration over having a plethora of (the wonderful) Bulbapedia tabs open trying to figure out just how to catch ’em all. This project wouldn’t be possible without the graciously and freely provided PokéAPI. Almost all of the data used here comes from PokéAPI.

Check out the Get In Touch page for more details on how to ask questions, report bugs, and all of that kind of stuff!
